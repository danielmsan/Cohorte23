STANDING TALL.

The world's tallest trees and more incredible destinations await in Humboldt County, California. Explore and plan your trip.

Welcome To Humboldt

It’s a land of giants and enchantment featuring the world famous Redwood National Park, Avenue of the Giants and Lost Coast, surprising cultural experiences in Victorian towns and seaports, and a wild and hairy side compliments of Bigfoot. With each step, you will find sights most extraordinary.

Enjoy your adventure. And always Stand Tall.
